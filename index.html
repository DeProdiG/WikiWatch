If this is the first time you seeing this game, better check it out now! It's a team-based action, free-to-play game that set in the optimistic future, where every match is the ultimate 5v5 battlefield brawl. "A future worth fighting for!"

The world's heroes: Overwatch features an international cast of powerful heroes with captivating personalities and backstories. There is whole story plot behind them which is worth reading too! The classes you can choose between are: Tank, Damage (DPS) or Support. Explore some of the most played in our wiki.
Game modes: Various rulesets that can be used to alter the way Overwatch 2 is played. Besides the five standard modes available in Competitive and Quick Play - Control, Escort, Flashpoints, Hybrid and Push - there are various other game modes available in the Arcade and Custom Games. During limited-time events, special game modes may also be available.
Outstanding maps: Travel the world and fight for the future in diverse locations around the globe. From the technological marvels of Busan to the snow-dusted streets of Toronto, every map has objectives to accomplish, secrets to uncover, and strategies to explore.
Cross-play is live now!: Cross-Play is now live in Overwatch, allowing players to group up and play together across platforms. Powered by one of the 2023 global Battle.net update, players from around the world can team up, regardless of their preferred platform.
